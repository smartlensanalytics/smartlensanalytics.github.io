DATA SCIENCE

Data Scientist

San Jose, CA

Responsible for building predictive bidding algorithms for paid online marketing by applying advanced analytics techniques such as data mining, data modeling, natural language processing and machine learning to develop and implement mathematical models and algorithms to solve optimization problems for internet software service company. Specifically, will prepare data by extracting and transforming large amount of raw data from structured and unstructured datasets into usable format and meaningful information; Perform statistical analysis and visualization to explore data; Formulate, implement and tune probabilistic models to optimize key business performance indicators; Develop analytical insights through interpreting model structure, visualizing model results and creating analytics reports to business stakeholders; Perform ad-hoc issue investigation and quantification of business impact, as well as forecasts on business movement; Conduct A/B tests and monitor performances.

Hours:

  • 40 hours per week

Education & Experience:

  • Requires a Ph.D. degree in Mathematics, Statistics or Computer Science plus one-year work experience in the job offered or closely related.
